About KIIT

KIIT (Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology) is a renowned university located in Bhubaneswar, Odisha, India. Founded in 1992, KIIT has grown into a multidisciplinary university offering diverse academic programs in engineering, management, law, medicine, and more.

Academics

KIIT is recognized for its excellence in academics and research. It offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs across various disciplines, fostering innovation and creativity among students.

Campus Life

The KIIT campus provides a vibrant environment for students with world-class infrastructure, modern amenities, and extracurricular activities. From state-of-the-art labs to sports complexes, KIIT ensures a holistic learning experience.
